A few caveats worth flagging:

- **Approximate dates** (~) are projections from past patterns where the official 2027 call hasn't been published yet. They're reliable enough for planning but not for last-minute submission.
- **JSPS programs** are submitted by the Japanese host researcher's institution to JSPS Tokyo — your collaborator handles submission, often with an institutional internal deadline a month earlier.
- **Villum Experiment** uses a lottery on expressions of interest; only ~300 of ~1100 EoIs proceed to the full March deadline.
- **DFF** has new rules from 2026: only one application per year per applicant across all DFF schemes, and applicants with 2+ active grants can't apply. Worth verifying with your research support office before picking which scheme to target.
- **NordForsk Nordic Research School** isn't a single recurring call — it's an instrument used in occasional thematic calls. The most recent (innovation/entrepreneurship 2025) is awarded; nothing comparable is currently open.
- **ECIU**: Aarhus University doesn't appear in current ECIU member lists — verify eligibility at AU before relying on this one.
