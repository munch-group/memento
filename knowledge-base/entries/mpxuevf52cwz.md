If $N$ is total pop size, $n$ is derived frequency, $k$ is number of derived lineages, and $s$ is the selection coeficient, then the derived coalescence rate is

$$
\frac{N-n}{N} \times \frac{(1+s) n}{N+sn} \times \frac{\binom{k}{2}}{\binom{n}{2} \binom{N-n}{2}}
$$

and the ancestral coalescence rate is

$$
\frac{N-n}{N} \times \frac{n}{N+sn} \times \frac{\binom{k}{2}}{\binom{n}{2} \binom{N-n}{2}}
$$
