Extrinsic / death-receptor pathway:

- `FAS` (CD95), `FASLG` (Fas ligand)
- `TNF`, `TNFRSF1A` (TNFR1)
- `TNFSF10` (TRAIL), `TNFRSF10A` (DR4), `TNFRSF10B` (DR5)
- `FADD`, `TRADD`, adaptors, DISC assembly
- `CASP8`, `CASP10`, initiator caspases
- `CFLAR` (c-FLIP), DISC inhibitor

Intrinsic / mitochondrial, BCL2 family:

Pro-apoptotic effectors: `BAX`, `BAK1`, `BOK`
BH3-only activators/sensitizers: `BID`, `BAD`, `BBC3` (PUMA), `PMAIP1` (NOXA), `BCL2L11` (BIM), `BIK`, `BMF`, `HRK`
Anti-apoptotic: `BCL2`, `BCL2L1` (BCL-xL), `BCL2L2` (BCL-w), `MCL1`, `BCL2A1` (A1/BFL-1)

Apoptosome & caspase cascade:

- `CYCS` (cytochrome c), `APAF1` — apoptosome
- `CASP9` — initiator
- `CASP3`, `CASP6`, `CASP7` — executioners
- `CASP2` — stress-linked initiator

Mitochondrial release factors:

- `DIABLO` (SMAC), `HTRA2` (Omi) — IAP antagonists
- `AIFM1` (AIF), `ENDOG` — caspase-independent death

IAPs (inhibitors of apoptosis):

- `XIAP`, `BIRC2` (cIAP1), `BIRC3` (cIAP2), `BIRC5` (survivin), `NAIP`

Upstream deciders / survival signaling:

- `TP53` — central stress integrator, transactivates `BAX`, `BBC3`, `PMAIP1`
- `MDM2`, `MDM4` — p53 negative regulators
- `CDKN2A` (p14ARF), `PTEN` — tumor-suppressor inputs
- `AKT1`/`AKT2`/`AKT3`, `NFKB1`/`RELA`, `MAPK8` (JNK) — survival vs. death signaling
