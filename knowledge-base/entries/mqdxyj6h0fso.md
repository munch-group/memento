> Duchenne muscular dystrophy (DMD) is a muscle-degenerating disease caused by mutations in the *DMD* gene, which encodes the dystrophin protein[1][2]. Utrophin (*UTRN*), the genetic and functional paralogue of *DMD*, is upregulated in some DMD patients[3][4][5].
