> *BMP15* encodes **bone morphogenetic protein 15**, a **secreted ligand** of the **TGF-beta superfamily** that regulates oocyte maturation and follicular development. The protein is processed from a preproprotein into disulfide-linked forms that can act as a **homodimer** or as a **heterodimer** with **GDF9**. Through activation of granulosa cells, it contributes to female gamete generation and receptor serine/threonine kinase signaling.
> BMP15 functions in the **extracellular region**, with localization also reported in the **endoplasmic reticulum lumen** and **cytoplasm**. As a TGF-beta family ligand, it binds TGF-beta receptors and leads to recruitment and activation of **SMAD** family transcription factors, thereby regulating gene expression. Protein expression is highest in the female reproductive system, consistent with its reproductive role.
> Pathogenic variants in *BMP15* are reported in **ovarian dysgenesis 2**. *BMP15* defects are also associated with **46,XX gonadal dysgenesis** and **premature ovarian failure**.
