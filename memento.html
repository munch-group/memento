Connect your knowledge base folder

Select your knowledge base folder. The app reads and writes entries as individual files on disk.

Works in Chrome and Edge. You can drag images onto cards.

Memento via GitHub

Load your knowledge base from GitHub. Entries are cached locally for fast repeat visits, and edits are committed straight back to the repo.

Needs a fine-grained Personal Access Token with Contents: Read and write on the repo. A read-only token still works — the app just stays read-only.
